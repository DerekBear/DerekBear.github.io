The Gaukler Family Wellness, Health and Physical Education Center is the fitness hub of campus.

As a student, your membership to the Wellness Center is included in your student fees–so head over and see what the hype is all about!

Facilities
The Gaukler Family Wellness Center includes:

a fitness area
multi-sport courts
a walking track
a swimming pool
a playground
a coffee and smoothie bar
The Wellness Center also houses the classrooms, labs, and offices for the Department of Kinesiology and Human Performance.
